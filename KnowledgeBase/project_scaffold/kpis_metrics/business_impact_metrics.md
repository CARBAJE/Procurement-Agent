# KPIs: Business Impact Metrics

> [!insight] The Core Value Proposition in Numbers
> The system replaces $10–25M/year in platform licensing costs for a $500M enterprise procurement operation, while delivering 70–90% cycle time reduction and 8–15% item cost savings through automated negotiation. At scale: a $1.2B addressable market today, growing to $4.2B by 2030.

## Business Impact Targets

| Metric | Target | Measurement Method |
|---|---|---|
| Procurement cycle time reduction | **70–90%** vs. baseline | Before/after comparison per procurement category |
| Cost savings from negotiation | **8–15%** avg. reduction | Agent-negotiated prices vs. list prices (90-day rolling window) |
| Platform licensing cost savings | **80–90%** reduction | Current Ariba/Coupa fees vs. open network operating costs |
| Procurement team productivity | **3×** throughput increase | Requests processed per FTE per month, before/after |
| Audit preparation time | **90%** reduction | Hours spent on audit prep, before/after |
